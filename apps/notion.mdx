## ‚öôÔ∏è Configuration

<Tip>
New App can be created at [Notion integrations page](https://www.notion.so/my-integrations).
</Tip>

Once you create the App, go to the "OAuth Domain & URIs" section and configure the below Redirect URL:

```
<https://backend.composio.dev/api/v1/auth-apps/notion/connection/oauth2/add>
```

After adding the Redirect URL, you will be redirected to another page where your Client ID and Client Secret will be displayed. Copy them for later use. On Composio's [supported apps](https://app.composio.dev/apps) page, find Notion and click "Enable". Paste your Client ID and Secret, then Save to connect Notion.
